Short Bio

I am a Senior Machine Learning Researcher at D-Wave Systems Inc. Prior to D-Wave, I was a University Research Associate (URA) at Simon Fraser University where I taught Master-level courses for SFU's Professional Master's program in Big Data and I did research on deep learning models for understanding human activities in videos. I obtained my Ph.D. degree from Simon Fraser University where I worked on large-scale internet video analysis frameworks under Prof. Greg Mori's supervision. I received my M.Sc. in Computing Science from SFU in 2011, and I earned my B.Sc. in Computer Engineering from Sharif University of Technology, Iran in 2009. In summer 2014, I did an internship at Google's Photo Search team, where I worked on deep convolutional neural networks for the image classification problem.
